Six-Factor Decision Intelligence

How We Analyze Every Product

Our engine evaluates products across six critical dimensions—no guesswork, no endorsements.

01

Concern Score

Quantitative risk indicator (0–100) based on ingredient composition and usage context.

02

Ingredient Purpose

Plain-language explanation of why each additive or chemical is used in the product.

03

Global Regulatory Status

Cross-country compliance comparison: FSSAI, FDA, EFSA, and other global authorities.

04

Additive Context

Scientific background and usage limits for preservatives, colorants, and stabilizers.

05

Personal Warnings

Age-specific, allergy-aware, and sensitivity-based alerts tailored to your profile.

06

News & Recalls

Real-time alerts on product recalls, regulatory updates, and verified safety news.
